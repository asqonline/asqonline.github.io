Who We Are

We are a team of professional research engineers, data scientists, business consultants, and educators. Our company offers two main stream of businesses: (1) Offshore Staffing and (2) Engineering Consultancy for nature and build assets. We are closely collaborating with Kyoto University, ETH Zurich, and Institute of Smart Cities and Management in Ho Chi Minh on conducting research and providing educational courses for students and future leaders.

Offshore Staffing

In partnering with EMAPTA (No. 1 BPO firm in the Philippines and Australia), ASQ offers highly qualified Vietnamese staffs for global Clients in various areas such as engineering design drafting and modelling, software development, paralegal services, and data analytics. Ultilizing EMAPTA's best practices and systems, ASQ team is confident to deliver quality works meeting Client's requirements and expectation.

Engineering Consultancy for Nature and Built Assets

We offer a number of unique engineering services that requires high level of professionalism and expertises including urban asset management, transportation engineering and impact assessment, urban risk and resilience analysis, flood study and modelling, technical and environmental due diligence, and data analytics.

Education & Training

In close collaboration with ETH Zurich, Kyoto University, and Institute of Smart City and Management at the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh, we are providing short courses on data analytics and asset management for students and professionals.
